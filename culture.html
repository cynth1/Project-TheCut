The event

Hear the merry voices of local children raised in song at a festive performance by primary school choirs from across the borough of Lewisham. Part of a year-round vocal programme from Lewisham Music, the Turning of the Year performance draws together choirs for a high-energy massed singing performance conducted by Clare Caddick.

The details

  • Location: Southbank Centre, The Clore Ballroom, Level 2
  • Date and time: Wed 12 Dec 2018, 6pm
  • Price: Free
